clairechenette Pigeon Parking Only ▶ 0
Day 24

May the Pigeon Series Commence!

♥ Love

clairechenette: On a second story telephone wire in the middle of Los Angeles hangs a sign that says "Pigeon Parking Only". 19 doves through a dusty window every day line themselves up there compactly. I can feel a pigeon eye on me as I scramble for a concrete spot. The wind blows and 19 tails tick up and down. What do they do up there? Are they sleeping? Are they preening? Nothing but ticking up and down when the wind blows, and 5 times an hour suddenly their governor directs a flustering flocking off flying down before fluttering chaotic whirring back to the pigeon parking line to sit still man by man side by side up high, and they'll do it again tomorrow And they'll do it again the day after And us too side by side below in our concrete line Tails Ticking up tick down when the wind bloooows ohs ohs ohs blows blow wind blow!
